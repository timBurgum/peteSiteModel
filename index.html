About

KJB Commercial provides investors, developers and occupiers with commercial property agency and consultancy services within the county of Worcestershire and beyond. We provide our clients with innovative solutions for both marketing and sourcing commercial property.
KJB Commercial also offers property consultancy services, enabling them to extract optimal value from their property assets.

Agency Services

We act on behalf of our clients in respect of the acquisition, sale and lettings of commercial properties. KJB Commercial deals with all categories of commercial property asset, with a particular focus on offices, industrial, retail and hospitality.

We also work closely with developers across the West Midlands in order to strategically acquire development opportunities.

Consultancy Services

We can provide practical and commercially driven advice in respect of lease re-gears, lease renewals, break clauses, rent reviews and development.
